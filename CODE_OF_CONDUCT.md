# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

We expect contributors to communicate respectfully, collaborate in good faith, and keep technical discussions focused on improving the project and supporting the people using it.

## Expected Behavior

- Be constructive, patient, and specific in feedback.
- Assume positive intent while still addressing risks directly.
- Respect privacy, especially when handling healthcare-adjacent data and operational details.
- Help keep reviews and discussions accessible to contributors with different levels of experience.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Deliberate disclosure of private data, credentials, or sensitive operational information
- Repeated bad-faith disruption of review, issue, or discussion threads

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it to the repository maintainers through a private security or maintainer contact channel instead of a public issue.
